Udaaan: Learn to Lead, Connect to Conquer

A dual-pathway platform offering world-class training (**Shiksha**) and direct access to global markets (**Vyapar**) for micropreneurs.

Start Learning (Shiksha) Connect to Market (Vyapar)

Udaaan Shiksha: Learning & Empowerment

Two pathways to elevate your business: foundational knowledge for local success and high-value skills for global growth.

Foundation Modules (Regional Focus)

Essential training tailored for rural micropreneurs, focusing on local markets, compliance, and basic digital usage.

  • Digital Literacy Basics: Using WhatsApp Business, online payments, and basic social media promotion.
  • Basic Accounting & Finance: Record keeping, calculating profits, and managing small expenses.
  • Local Scheme Awareness: How to access state-level microfinance and self-help group (SHG) funding.

High-Value Modules (Global Focus)

Advanced modules to scale your business, focusing on international standards, intellectual property, and establishing a powerful brand identity.

  • Crafting Your USP (Unique Selling Proposition): Define what makes your product unique and marketable globally.
  • Global Quality & Branding: Understanding international quality certifications and building a trustworthy brand narrative.
  • Export Documentation Essentials: Mastering IEC code, custom procedures, and shipping paperwork.

Udaaan Vyapar: Global Market Connect

Connect your micropreneur-led business directly to global industrial buyers and international e-commerce platforms.

Global Industry Matching (B2B)

Direct connection service to large international companies looking for unique, sustainable products from India.

Export Logistics Support

Guided assistance with packaging standards, customs clearance, shipping arrangements, and international trade compliance.

Digital Commerce Exposure (B2C)

Platform integration with international e-commerce sites (Etsy, Amazon Global) and training on cross-border sales.
